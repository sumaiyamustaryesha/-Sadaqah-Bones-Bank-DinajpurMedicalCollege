WHO WE ARE

The Sadaqah Bone Bank is a Shariah-compliant initiative led by female medical students residing at Dr. Toyubur Rahman Girls’ Hostel, Dinajpur Medical College.

Recognizing that the commercialization of the human body violates its dignity and is strictly prohibited in Islam, this initiative was established to align medical education with Islamic principles. Many students are required to study human bones as part of their academic journey; however, purchasing such materials raises ethical and religious concerns.

For students who already possess bone sets, the most meaningful and rewarding path is to transform this resource into Sadaqah Jariyah (ongoing charity) by donating it for the benefit of junior students. Our role is to act as a facilitator—encouraging a culture of Sadaqah (charity) and Amanah (trust) within our campus and hostels.

We seek no material gain. Our reward lies with Allah, and our success is reflected in fostering a cleaner, more ethical educational environment.

Our Mission

Advancing Medical Excellence through Integrity and Taqwa. The study of human anatomy is an essential foundation of medical education. However, it presents a significant ethical challenge when it involves the commercial trade of human remains. The mission of the Sadaqah Bone Bank is to offer a Shariah-compliant alternative.

How It Works

The Sadaqah Bone Bank operates as a coordination platform. System built on trust, responsibility, and mutual respect: Registry of Donors, Connecting Students, Custodian Responsibility, Perpetual Charity.

✔ উপরিউক্ত স্টেটমেন্ট এর সাথে একমত হয়ে যারা সাদাকা বোনস-ব্যাংক হতে বোনস নিতে চান তারা নিচের ফর্মটি পূরণ করবেন। চূড়ান্ত গ্রহীতা যাচাই বাছাই করে নির্বাচন করা হবে।
