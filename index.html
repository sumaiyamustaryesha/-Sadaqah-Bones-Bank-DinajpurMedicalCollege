ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

Sadaqah • Ethics • Medical Integrity

Who We Are

The Sadaqah Bone Bank is a Shariah compliant initiative that works for the female medical students of Dr. Taiyobur Rahman Girls’ Hostel, Dinajpur Medical College .

This initiative aligns medical education with Islamic principles. As commercialization of the human body violates its dignity and is prohibited in Islam, we promote ethical alternatives based on Sadaqah (charity) and Amanah (trust).

This organization is non-political and non-profitable. Our reward is with Allah, and our success lies in building a more ethical and responsible educational environment.

Our Mission

Advancing medical excellence through integrity and Taqwa. We aim to provide a Shariah-compliant alternative to the ethical challenges in anatomical education by fostering voluntary donation, trust-based systems, and responsible academic practice.

How It Works

  • Registry of voluntary donors within ethical guidelines
  • Connection between medical students through structured system
  • Custodian responsibility ensuring dignity and respect
  • Promotion of perpetual charity (Sadaqah Jariyah)
